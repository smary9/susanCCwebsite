About Me

Susan Algaze

Susan has worked for over 20 years as an essay editor for published, peer-reviewed scholarly articles and academic books, successful grant applications, and college and scholarship essays. She has helped many students obtain college admissions to such schools as Stanford, Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Columbia, Cornell, The University of Chicago, MIT, Duke, UPenn (CAS, Engineering and Wharton), UC Berkeley, UCLA, UVA, UNC, Pomona, Swarthmore, Grinnell, Vassar, Wellesley, Williams, and Amherst, among others. She has also helped some of her clients receive generous merit aid, including full tuition scholarships at such schools as Vanderbilt and Washington University of Saint Louis.

Susan Algaze has an M.A. in Speech, Language, and Hearing Sciences from San Diego State University and a B.S. in Languages and Linguistics from Georgetown University.
