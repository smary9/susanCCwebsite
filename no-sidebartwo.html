Services

We are able to advise students throughout the entire process - from constructing lists of strategically chosen reach, match, and safety schools, finding and applying for institutional and private merit scholarships, communicating with admissions representatives to show "demonstrated interest," providing help with need or merit-based financial aid and scholarships, filling out college applications and Financial Aid forms, and in leveraging their choices by taking advantage of rolling admissions, Early and Restrictive Early Action, as well as Early and Regular Decision programs, how to handle deferrals and waitlists in order to enhance their outcomes.

We specialize in helping each student craft creative application essays and put together interesting profiles that will, in turn, help them put their best foot forward and stand out in in the eyes of college admissions committees.
